# Privacy Policy for TTMALL Sync App

**Last Updated:** February 4, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Our app ("the App") is designed to connect your Shopify store with TTMALL. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use the App.

## 2. Information We Collect
When you install the App, we automatically access certain information from your Shopify account:
* **Product Information:** Titles, descriptions, and prices to sync to TTMALL.
* **Order Information:** Order details to manage cross-platform fulfillment.
* **Inventory Data:** Stock levels to ensure real-time synchronization.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use the information collected from your store to:
* Synchronize listings and inventory between Shopify and TTMALL.
* Provide customer support and ensure system stability.
* We **DO NOT** sell your personal or business data to third parties.

## 4. Data Retention
We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide you with our services and as described in this Privacy Policy.
